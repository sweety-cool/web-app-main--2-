Help - Ongoing projects Machinist

By using the filter button, you can easily identify:
My current orders:

These are the commands you have won, so why the funds were paid by your client. So you can be machined and send the order to your customer.

My finalized orders:

These are orders for which your client has released the funds to your account Usineur.fr. So you were paid for these commands.

My Offers:

These are all the ads on which you made an offer and for which you have not yet been chosen.

In the menu "Other:
My offer has been accepted but the client has not ordered by pouring funds:

You have been chosen for this machining application but the command is not yet passed because the funds were not paid. You do not have to start the machining work. We recommend you to send a message to your potential customer to inform him that you need the funds to be paid to start machining. Customers are sometimes not aware and your message will encourage them to disburse funds.

My finalized orders that have not yet received the assessment:

Too bad you did not receive an assessment for your work that will enrich your profile. Send a message to your customer requesting an assessment, he probably forgot.
